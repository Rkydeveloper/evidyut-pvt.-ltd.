Diagnostix

An easy to set-up, non-invasive data acquisition tool for independent analysis of vehicle performance and telemetric data. Useful for cost-effective vehicle design and compliance preparation. Pairs with any android device with Bluetooth 2.1 or greater and works seamlessly with the bundled app.

Use Cases:

Diagnostix empowers you to become your own vehicle detective. By offering real-time telemetry data, it gives you a comprehensive overview of your vehicle's health. You can proactively identify and address potential faults before they become bigger problems. Planning trips becomes a breeze with Diagnostix's accurate range estimation and optimization tools, and exploring new routes is aided by its terrain and track mapping functionalities.

Specifications:

  • 3 channels for precise current and voltage measurement
  • Voltage sensing up to 600V DC and 1100V AC
  • Non-contact current sense up to 200A
  • Battery State of Charge (SoC) monitoring
  • 3-axis gyroscope and accelerometer for motion and orientation tracking
  • 2 temperature sensors for thermal monitoring
  • 4 digital input channels for RPM and various sensor applications
  • 6 digital input channels for on/off detection
  • Data output via Serial Port (UART) and Bluetooth streaming
