RetroKit®

RetroKit® converts an autorickshaw running on Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) consuming Petrol or Diesel into an Electric Vehicle (EV) in just 2 hours. Our solution is cost-effective as it retains the majority of the original autorickshaw components, making it an environmentally friendly and sustainable option.

Ease of Installation

This instrument cluster boasts seamless installation. It utilizes existing engine mounting locations, eliminating the need for additional drilling or modifications. A trained professional can complete the installation within two hours.

Novelty of RetroKit® Geartrain

The RetroKit® geartrain stands out for its innovative design and exceptional adaptability. This unique gearbox boasts compatibility with a wide range of existing differential gears and motor types, allowing for seamless integration into various vehicle models.

Installation Procedure

The RetroKit® geartrain installation prioritizes ease and accessibility. Authorized and trained retrofitting agents will handle the process right at your existing autorickshaw mechanic's garage, minimizing disruption. These agents will be equipped with specialized installation and calibration tools to ensure optimal performance and quality assurance. Furthermore, most wear-prone geartrain components are designed to be compatible with existing rickshaw spare parts, simplifying maintenance. After-sales service will be exclusively provided by our trained retrofitting agents to ensure the best results.
